    Our Story Begins

    The Moffat Bay Lodge opened its doors in the spring of 1944. An instant hit with locals and tourists alike, it quickly became known for its excellent food, breathtaking views, and unforgettable marine expeditions. Founded by John Lawrence and his wife, Patty, and passed down through the generations, Moffat Bay Lodge remains family-owned and operated today.

    Moffat Bay Today

    Over the years, Moffat Bay Lodge has been renovated and updated to host more guests and offer modern luxuries, while still maintaining its reputation for excellence. Currently owned and operated by Vanessa Lawrence and family, Moffat Bay prioritizes creating unforgettable moments for all guests—adventurers and loungers alike. This year, we added two new offerings: scuba diving tours and the on-site Moffat Bay Spa. Guests have been raving about the seaweed wrap!

    Our Commitment to Excellence

    Moffat Bay Lodge understands that a great stay and memorable experience are the heart of our business. We offer knowledgeable concierge services to help our guests make the most of their visit. We are committed to excellence in everything we do, which is why we invest in sustainable and ethical practices, including ocean-friendly excursions, farm-to-table dining, and education reimbursement for our talented employees.
