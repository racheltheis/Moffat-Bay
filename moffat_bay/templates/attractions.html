Attractions

Kayaking Tours

Moffat Bay Lodge offers kayak rentals and guided tours that explore the natural wonders of Moffat Bay's shoreline. Kayak guided tours are available for all levels of expertise and fitness levels. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced kayaker, our expert guides will tailor the perfect experience. Along the way, you can spot local wildlife, including dolphins, pelicans, and seals. Tours also provide opportunities to learn about Moffat Bay's best-kept secrets, making it a perfect combination of adventure and serenity. Reservations for rentals and tours can be made at the front desk or by phone.

Hiking Trails

Immerse yourself in the quiet beauty of Moffat Bay's wilderness with our 25 miles of hiking trails. Our hiking trails range from .5 to 7 miles, and in difficulty from easy to challenging. Along our trails, you can find incredible sights and stops, like a natural hotspring, a luscious waterfall, and a stunning cave system with fossilized walls. Trail maps can be found at our front desk, and guided cave tours are available daily at 1 p.m.

Whale Watching

Moffat Bay Lodge has been a proud partner of Hendrickson Whale Watching for over 50 years, offering seasonal tours from May to October. Guests can experience an unforgettable glimpse of ocean life, spotting not only whales but also dolphins, seals, and even sharks. Tours run Thursday through Monday, starting at 10 a.m., and we recommend booking at least one week in advance as spots fill quickly. For pricing and reservations, please visit our front desk or give us a call.

Scuba Diving

Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced diver, Moffat Bay’s scuba diving excursions are designed for all skill levels. Our team of certified trainers and guides provides expert instruction, support, and safety oversight for every guest. Beneath the waves, Moffat Bay reveals stunning natural reefs, vibrant coral, and a rich diversity of marine life. Safety is our top priority—guests who are not already certified receive a complimentary diving course and competency test before their first dive. Our diving team leads dives from June to September, seven days a week. Reservations can be made by phone are at the front desk.
